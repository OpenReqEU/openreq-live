Speeds up Requirements Engineering

We did most of the heavy lifting for you to provide a default workspace that incorporates our custom components. Additionally, InnoSensr takes advantage of smart techniques based on intelligent group decision-making algorithms which assist stakeholders during the complete requirements engineering process. The intended purpose of InnoSensr is to improve the quality of requirements-engineering-related decisions and to reduce the time effort for making smart decisions.

group

User Experience Focused

By utilizing tailor-made modern UI elements and state-of-the art design principles, we were able to create an application that incorporates components and animations that provide more feedback to users. Visual feedback is presented to the users in such a way that they can easily understand decision issues which typically occur in group decisions making processes. This allows stakeholders of your project to focus on the work that really matters to your business.

Easy to work with

We provided detailed documentation and integrated assistance tools into InnoSensr to help new users to get started quickly. We are also always open to feedback and requests a stakeholder may has regarding InnoSensr. Furthermore, group decisions in InnoSensr are supported by sophisticated recommendation algorithms to foster stakeholders in the development and maintenance of their requirements models.
